# Why this skill exists

The common ailment when an LLM writes a SKILL.md is treating it as a manual — long narration, rules mixed with examples, trigger conditions buried in the body (the body does not participate in triggering). This diagnosis separates the quantifiable parts (char count, line count, prefix keywords, third person) from the parts that need judgment (what counts as bloat, which section belongs in scripts/): hard rules are judged mechanically, soft judgments are made against the references.
